Description of design efforts:
Throughout the project I will be working on implementing the GPS module. The focus of this week was to research GPS operation and possible GPS modules to use. GPS receivers use a satellite constellation of at least 24 satellites to calculate potision at a given time. To do this calculation accurately the receiver must be able to lock onto 4 satellites. The satellite configuration ensures that this is always possible [1].

Figure 1: Constellation of Orbiting Satellites [1]
Only basic functionality of a GPS is needed for the project to receive coordinate and alititude data. Since the functionality requried for the GPS module is pretty basic, the feature that I determined to be most important is power consumption. The goggles will be battery operated for hours at a time between charging. I have been looking at small modules with low consumption. Some modules, like the MTK3339 by Adafruit, have a sleep mode that can be activated to lower consumption further [2]. Since the GPS will only be actively receiving when requested by the user, this could be a useful fucntion.

The next step is to aqcuire the physical GPS module and begin experimenting with its functionality.
